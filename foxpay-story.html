FoxPay Ecosystem & Tokens

FOX is the core utility token inside FoxPay. Players earn FOX by completing daily tasks, tapping, watching videos, and participating in CPA missions. GFOX represents the Web3 governance and long-term blockchain vision of our studio.

Mining Tier System

Packages enhance your mining speed. Higher tier packages (Silver, Gold, VIP) increase your daily energy capacity, profit-per-hour, and unlock high-tier World Cup betting limits.

Live Fixed Odds Predictions

Our World Cup 2026 prediction module allows players to place bets on live real-world fixtures. Match odds (e.g. Favorite x1.10, Draw x3.00, Underdog x2.00) automatically calculate your payout in FOX.

Sustainable Withdrawals & Affiliates

FoxPay operates under transparent capping rules to guarantee economic sustainability. All player balances can be withdrawn directly to BEP-20 / USDT crypto wallets via NOWPayments.
